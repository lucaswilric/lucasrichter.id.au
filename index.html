I'm Lucas Wilson-Richter. I'm a coder by day and a classical baritone by night. I read books, articles, blogs and tweets. I sing by myself and with a few different choirs. I build web apps that use RSS. I listen to lots of music that features pianos. I use Ubuntu, Windows, OSX and Android. I tread the boards in light operas. I tweet, and I've made my own tools for blogging and link-blogging. I occasionally indulge in scene shifting and stage management. I drink coffee every day. I drink wine and whiskey some days. I'm passionate about using technology to get information to the people who need it. I like to learn and play with shiny things.
